Discover Sri Vasavi Institute: Where Education Meets Excellence!

Sri Vasavi Institute of Engineering & Technology (SVIET), established in 2007 by visionary industrialists, is an autonomous institute dedicated to holistic education. Located in the serene Nandamuru, Pedana, Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh, SVIET blends knowledge, innovation, and a human touch to nurture talent for the modern world.

  • Approved by: AICTE, New Delhi
  • Affiliated to: JNTUK, Kakinada
  • Accredited by: NAAC
  • NBA Accredited Departments: CSE, ECE & ME
  • Status: Autonomous Institute
Vision:

To be a center of excellence in technical education, fostering innovation and empowering students to become responsible professionals and leaders.

Mission:

To provide value-based education, nurture talent, and promote research and entrepreneurship that meet the needs of industry and society.
